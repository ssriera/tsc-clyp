How does Clyp work?

Upload your audio file (or record something new) and Clyp turns it into a shareable short link which can be shared or embedded anywhere.

How do I upload audio to Clyp?

You can upload an audio file to Clyp on the homepage. Just drage and drop your file into the upload box, or click the upload box to find the file on your computer.

How do I record audio with Clyp?

You can record audio directly to Clyp by pressing the big orange record button on ur homepage (only available in certain browsers). You can also record audio through our iOS and Android apps.

How is this different than SoundCloud?

1) There's no account required to record or upload audio. 2) It's ridiculously easy to upload or record audio with Clyp. Just press the big orange record button or drop in your audio file to the upload box on the homepage. 3) Anti-cencorship. Everyone has a right to be themselves. As long as your content is legal, we won't judge. 4) Open API. Developers can utilize our badass API to add audio uploading to their site or app.

How do I log in?

To log in on our website just hit the Log In link in the top right of the navigation menu. You will be prompted to log in with your email address and password, or through Facebook. To log in on the app, hit the big orange log in button under the Profile tab and you will be given the same log in options as the website.

What is a private Clyp?

A private Clyp will not be searchable and will never show up on any of our curated lists. We respect your privacy. However, anyone you share the link with will be able to access your Clyp.

Are there any upload/recording limitations?

We limit audio uploads by file size, not by file length. Some audio file types are larger in nature compared to others. For example, a 128kbps mp3 file is going to be much smaller in size when compared to a .WAV file. The file size limit per upload is 100mb, which works out to about an hour if you're uploading an mp3 file.

What types of audio files do you accept?

We accept the following audio file types: mp3, ogg, m4a, wav, aiff, aif and 3gpp.

How do I share my Clyp to Facebook?

To share a Clyp to Facebook, just hit the Facebook icon on the player page. A popup will appear after you hit the Facebook icon and you can say whatever you want about the Clyp in your post. Alternatively, you can just copy and paste the short URL into your Facebook status. On the apps, you have the option of sharing directly to Facebook whenever you're adding in a title and description to your Clyp - just tap the small Facebook icon. You can also share it once you've uploaded your Clyp by tapping the share arrow icon and selecting Facebook.

How do I share my Clyp to Twitter?

It's basically the same approach as Facebook. You can click the Twitter logo on the player page or copy the link directly into a Tweet. You can also share via the apps with the same approach.

How does your widget work?

To use the widget, go to the player page of the desired Clyp and navigate to the advanced tab on the right. Under this tab you are given the markup to embed a Clyp on your website or on reddit. Simply copy the code and paste it wherever you want.

How do I embed a Clyp into Reddit?

Follow the steps above and choose "Markdown Link" and then paste this into your reddit post.
